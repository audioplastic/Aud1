About

    Aud1 was created by Dr. Nick clark to address many of the user requests related to the original BioAid app. Nick was a key member of the original academic research team that devised and refined the core BioAid algorithm. Aud1 has been developed to unleash more of the potential of this research, while simultaneously advancing the technology.
