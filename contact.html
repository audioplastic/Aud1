Contact

    Users of Aud1 are encouraged to get in contact with any feedback about their experiences with the app. The work involved in creating and updating Aud1 is motivated by user Feedback, and is essential to future developments.

    One of the best ways to get in contact is via the new facebook page and write a message. Posting messages here will alow any replies to be seen by a wider audience. Hitting the “Like” button on the page will allow you to receive updates whenever there are any exciting developments on the way.

    If you’d rather contact me directly, your welcome to email me using nick@aud1.com.

    If you’re a twitter user, I’ll post any updates related to Aud1 via my twitter handle audioplastic.
